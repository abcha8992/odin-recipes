DESCRIPTION

This recipe has been called the best by many and has thousands of 5-star reviews! It uses simple ingredients that you likely have on hand, and it packs big flavor! This is an easy lasagna recipe. It requires just one pan, one bowl, and a 9x13 baking dish. It reheats well and can be made ahead of time. You can freeze lasagna either before or after baking.

INGREDIENTS

  • ground meat (beef and/or sausage)lasagna noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • ricotta cheese
  • mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • eggs
  • parsley
  • Italian seasoning
  • Optional : Some recipes may also include vegetables like onions, garlic, or bell peppers.

STEPS

  1. Prepare the meat sauce - Brown ground beef and sausage with onion and garlic in a skillet.Drain excess fat.Add pasta sauce, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, and other seasonings and simmer to thicken,according to Spend With Pennies
  2. Prepare the cheese mixture - Combine ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, parsley, and egg in a bowl.
  3. Cook the lasagna noodles - Boil lasagna noodles in salted water until al dente, according to package directions.Drain and rinse under cold water to prevent sticking.
  4. Assemble the lasagna - Preheat oven to 375°F.Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.Top with a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture. Add another layer of meat sauce.Repeat the layers of noodles, ricotta mixture, and meat sauce.Finish with a top layer of noodles and the remaining meat sauce. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Cook the lasagna - Cover the baking dish loosely with aluminum foil. Bake for 45 minutes at 375°F.Remove the foil and bake for another 15 minutes or until the top is golden brown and bubbly.Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before serving.
  6. That's it! Give yourself a pat on the back and enjoy your Lasagna!
